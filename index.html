About Us

"Any fool can write a code that a computer understand. A good programmer write code that humans can understand"

-JOHN JOHNSON

Programming & Webix Club, IIITDM Jabalpur is a team of Enthusiastic learners who find their passion in programming and promote coding culture in our institute, we develop unique and splendid Websites, Apps and Graphics. We work and learn in a Team and explore the world of World Wide Web to its vast horizons. Since its inception, we constantly supports the "Digital India" and "Skill India" campaigns of our Country, that promotes us engineers to develop, design and create a whole new era of Startup Industry. Programming & Webix Club also provides support in the design and development of different college websites including - Tarang, Abhikalpan and Gusto. Finally, we believe in "LEARN BY DOING" and that is what makes us different!!

What do we do..

At Programming & webix Club, our sessions include discussions on programming topics, and various topic related to Web Application development and Graphic Designing. We teach various programming languages, provide knowledge about competitions worldwide, Algorithms and mathematical tips and tricks. We aquaint students with various websites and best learning material online.We keep ourselves updated with the latest technologies in the current scenario. Come join us and explore a whole new world with us!!



Events

"Everyday life is like programming, I guess. If you love something you can put beauty into it"

-DONALD KNUTH

Hackathon

Hackathon is a 24 hour event in which students develop web apps or android apps based on the given themes

The goal of a hackathon is to learn new hting in a team and create usable software. It is one of the biggest event of the year organised every and with a huge participation and filled with excitement, tiredness, rush and passion.

Online Coding Competiton

A mind sport organised on hackerearth, codechef or hackerrank to motivate students about competitve coding.

It is one of the oldest event of our club organised many time consisting of logical and mathematical problems to the contestants and they are required to solve it using any programming language.

Web Mockup

A one day competition where participants are required to desing a website of one or two page showing their web develpoment skills and the creativity in the way they present the information.

CryptoCracker

A short contest consisting of aptitude, logical and mathematical daily life problems. It not only test your logical thinking but also test that how fast can you understand the problem and solve it. This competition recieves a huge participation as it does not require a special programming knowledge and even the students from other disciplines participate in it.

Game Jam

A 24-Hour game development contest, in short HACKATHON FRO GAMES. Participants design a game generally computer game.The type of technology can vary depending on the type of game being developed, and among the different disciplines involved. A contest to learn and design something which is liked by almost all of us.
